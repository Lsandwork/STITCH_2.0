# Sage Pocket Dino

A seated green dinosaur with a rounded head, cream belly, small arms, tail, and soft dorsal spikes.

## Safety
For children under 3, embroider eyes instead of using safety eyes. Secure every part with multiple passes. This is a decorative handmade toy, not a sleep-safe infant item.

## Materials
- DK cotton or acrylic:
  - Sage 220 yd
  - Dark sage 40 yd
  - Cream 45 yd
  - Blush scrap
- 3 mm hook
- 10 mm safety eyes or black embroidery floss
- Polyester stuffing
- Needle, marker, pins

## Gauge
Not critical. Work tightly enough that stuffing does not show.

## Head and body - continuous spiral
With sage:

Rnd 1: 6 sc in MR. 6
Rnd 2: Inc around. 12
Rnd 3: (Sc, inc) around. 18
Rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) around. 24
Rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) around. 30
Rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) around. 36
Rnd 7: (5 sc, inc) around. 42
Rnds 8-15: Sc around. 42

Place eyes between Rnds 11-12, 8 stitches apart.

Rnd 16: (5 sc, dec) around. 36
Rnd 17: (4 sc, dec) around. 30
Rnd 18: (3 sc, dec) around. 24
Stuff head firmly.

Rnd 19: (2 sc, dec) around. 18
Rnd 20: Sc around. 18
Rnd 21: (2 sc, inc) around. 24
Rnd 22: (3 sc, inc) around. 30
Rnd 23: (4 sc, inc) around. 36
Rnds 24-34: Sc around. 36

Rnd 35: (4 sc, dec) around. 30
Rnd 36: (3 sc, dec) around. 24
Stuff body, keeping base flat.
Rnd 37: (2 sc, dec) around. 18
Rnd 38: (Sc, dec) around. 12
Rnd 39: Dec around. 6
Close.

## Muzzle
Cream:
Rnd 1: Ch 7. Sc in second ch and next 4, 3 sc in last; work opposite side, sc in next 4, 2 sc in final. 14
Rnd 2: Inc, 4 sc, inc in next 3, 4 sc, inc in next 2. 20
Rnds 3-4: Sc around.
Fasten off with sewing tail. Lightly stuff and sew centered below eyes. Embroider nostrils and a small smile.

## Belly patch
Cream:
Ch 9.
Row 1: Sc in second ch and across. 8
Rows 2-10: Ch 1, sc across.
Row 11: Dec, 4 sc, dec. 6
Row 12: Dec, 2 sc, dec. 4
Sc evenly around patch. Sew to front body with top below muzzle.

## Legs - make 2
Sage:
Rnd 1: 6 sc MR.
Rnd 2: Inc around. 12
Rnd 3: (Sc, inc) around. 18
Rnds 4-7: Sc around.
Rnd 8: (Sc, dec) around. 12
Stuff.
Rnds 9-11: Sc around.
Fasten off. Pin low on body in seated position and sew.

## Arms - make 2
Rnd 1: 6 sc MR.
Rnd 2: (Sc, inc) around. 9
Rnds 3-8: Sc around.
Lightly stuff only lower half. Flatten and sc through both layers. Sew at body sides angled toward belly.

## Tail
Rnd 1: 6 sc MR.
Rnd 2: (Sc, inc) around. 9
Rnds 3-5: Sc around.
Rnd 6: (2 sc, inc) around. 12
Rnds 7-9: Sc around.
Rnd 10: (3 sc, inc) around. 15
Rnds 11-13: Sc around.
Rnd 14: (4 sc, inc) around. 18
Rnds 15-17: Sc around.
Stuff gradually. Flatten open end slightly and sew to lower back, curving tail to one side.

## Dorsal spikes - make 7
Dark sage:
Ch 5.
Sl st in second ch, sc in next, hdc in next, dc in last.
Fasten off leaving tail.
Make two small spikes with ch 4 and five standard spikes with ch 5. Sew from crown down back onto tail.

## Cheeks
With blush, make two small horizontal satin stitches below eyes.

## Finishing
Shape with pins before final sewing. Add stuffing through any soft area before fully closing seams.

## Troubleshooting
**Head flops:** Pack neck area firmly and add several reinforcing stitches between head and shoulders.
**Toy leans:** Position legs symmetrically and flatten base while stuffing.
**Gaps show:** Use smaller hook.
