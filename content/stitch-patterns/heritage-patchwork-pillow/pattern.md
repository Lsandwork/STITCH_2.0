# Heritage Patchwork Pillow

A removable 18 in cushion cover made from sixteen small motifs on the front and an envelope-style solid back.

## Materials
- Worsted cotton or wool blend, 650 yd total in 7 colors
- 4 mm hook
- 18 in pillow insert
- 5 buttons, 0.75 in
- Needle and markers

## Gauge
One blocked motif = 4.25 in.
16 dc x 9 rows = 4 in in back panel.

## Front motifs - make 16
Use four motif styles, four of each.

### Motif A - classic granny
R1: MR, four groups of 3 dc separated by ch 2.
R2-R4: Work standard granny-square increases in corners and 3 dc in side spaces.

### Motif B - solid square
R1: MR, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 2; repeat four corners.
R2-R4: Dc in every stitch, working (2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc) in corners.

### Motif C - sunburst
R1: 16 dc in MR.
R2: Puff in each stitch separated by ch 1.
R3: 3 dc in each ch-1 space.
R4: Shape square with alternating sc, hdc, dc, tr and corner tr groups.

### Motif D - flower
Use the first four rounds of the Sunset Market Bag flower square, adjusted to finish at 4.25 in.

Block all motifs to identical dimensions.

## Front assembly
Arrange motifs in a 4 x 4 grid. Join with mattress seam for an heirloom look. Work one sc round around the completed front, placing 3 sc in each corner.

## Back lower panel
Ch 70.
Row 1: Dc in fourth ch and across. 67 dc.
Continue dc rows until panel measures 18 in wide x 11.5 in tall. Adjust stitch count to exact width based on gauge.

Button band:
Work 6 rows sc BLO across top edge. On Row 3 make 5 evenly spaced buttonholes: ch 2, skip 2.

## Back upper panel
Ch 70. Work dc rows until 18 x 10 in. Finish lower edge with 4 rows sc BLO.

Sew buttons centered opposite lower-panel buttonholes.

## Assembly
1. Block all three panels.
2. Lay front WS up.
3. Place upper back RS up, aligned with top.
4. Place lower back RS up, aligned with bottom, overlapping upper panel by approximately 3.5 in.
5. Pin all layers.
6. Join through all layers with sc around, placing 3 sc in corners.
7. Optional final round: reverse sc.

Insert pillow through envelope opening and button closed.

## Troubleshooting
**Front larger than back:** Add border rows to back panels or reduce front border.
**Corners curl:** Steam lightly and let cool pinned square.
**Insert looks flat:** Use a 20 in insert for a fuller 18 in cover.
